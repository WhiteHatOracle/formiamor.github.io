A Poem For My Love

In the soft embrace of twilight's grace, I find my heart in a tranquil space. Underneath the gentle, starry sky, I'm captivated by your loving sigh., Your eyes, like pools of calm, serene, Reflect a love that's seldom seen. Your touch, so tender, a soothing balm, In your gentle arms, I find my calm.

Your smile, a radiant, gentle glow, A warmth that makes my heart aglow. With every whisper of your name, My love for you remains the same. In every single way, In the quiet moments, hand in hand, We walk together on the endless sand. A gentle love, so sweet and true, It's only found when I'm with you.

Like a gentle breeze that rustles the trees, You fill my life with boundless ease. In your love's embrace, I'm forever content, For in your gentle love, my heart's intent.

So, my dear, in this verse I send, My love for you that will never end. With a gentle touch and a love so true, I'm forever thankful for the gentle you.
